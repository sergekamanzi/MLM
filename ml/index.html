Frequently Asked Questions

Is MYM Hub free to use?

Yes, basic features are free. Premium services like one-on-one counseling may have additional costs.

How is my privacy protected?

We use advanced encryption and strict privacy policies to ensure your data and conversations remain confidential.

Can I use MYM Hub anonymously?

Yes, you can use a pseudonym for community features. However, verified information is required for counseling sessions.
